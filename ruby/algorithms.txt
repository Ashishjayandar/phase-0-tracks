Algorithm for using an elevator.
	-Go to the elevator
	-Depending on whether you need to go up or down press the corresponding button and wait for the lift.
	-When the lift comes, wait for the people to exit. If there is no one else exiting the elevator
			- step inside the elevator.
	-press the button to the floor to which you need to go.
	-once the floor is reached and the doors are open.
		- exit the elevator without pushing the person in front of you.
